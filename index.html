A remotely controllable cat taunting robot. Includes camera for remote monitoring, laser for classic taunting, speaker for audio tauting, as well as local control with Wii remote via bluetooth.

HARDWARE

Here are some details about the hardware. The overall parts are shown below.

The two custom boards are shown below. The one on the right is for general purpose power switching using three transistor controlled via the Rpi GPIO pins. The one on the left is also for power switching, but also includes three pots which act as current limiting resistors for attached LEDs. This allows for custom trimming of individual LED brightness.

A Raspberry Pi v1 camera module and super bright white LED are mounted on one set of servos.

A LED laser is mounted on the other set of servos.
